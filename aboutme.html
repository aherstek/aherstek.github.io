Fun facts?

The world's most famous 'wabbit'

A timeline....
  • 1940: Made his official debut on July 27, in a short film "A Wild Hare"
  • 1943: Became a US Navy marine to show his support to soldiers in WWII
  • 1945-1961: Voted #1 cartoon character in the world (16 years in a row!)
  • 1985: Gains his own star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame
  • 1996: Played basketball with Michael Jordan in "Space Jam"
  • 1997: Became the first cartoon character to appear on a US stamp
  • 2012: Appeared in 175+ films (more than any other chartoon character!)
  • 2023: Bugs bunny turns 83 years young!
